Some words:

 

I've been organizing this web page to make easy to you know a little bit of my life and what I'm currently working on. Since Aug 15th I'm working as Post-Doc Associated in Nuclear Science & Engineering at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) in Cambridge/Boston area. My research field is numerical simulation of two-phase flow in nuclear power plants. During my doctoral studies, I'd been working in the discretization of the Navier-Stokes's equations with a special modelling to describe intefacial forces in two-phase flows through the Finite Element Method. The in-house numerical code is designed by modern and flexible object-oriented languages: (C++) and (Python) which allows easy maintenance and further development. The aim of the Post-Doc position is to benchmark the local experimental database using a commercial front-tracking code available in the laboratory.

 

Talking about my personal life, I like to play sports. I enjoy to play tennis, soccer, volleyball, basket, cycling and swimming. I also like to play the acoustic guitar in my free time and record some funny things with my computer webcam.

 

So, enjoy my webpage and contact me if you need any further information! :)
